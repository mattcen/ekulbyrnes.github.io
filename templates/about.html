My name is Luke. I enjoy making websites and teaching others to do the same. I created ScoutHack to provide opportunities for young people to get an insight into real tech experiences while they have access to great mentors.

ScoutHack is a fun and exciting program that teaches young people to build a simple website using HTML, CSS, and Python. JavaScript is avoided at all costs - website development shouldn't start with torture.

Want to learn more about ScoutHack?
Check out our website to see what we're all about! ScoutHack is available for Scouts, Venturers, and Rovers who are active members of Scouts Victoria.
